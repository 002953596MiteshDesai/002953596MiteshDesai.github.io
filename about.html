Welcome to our non-profit website, giving all the information about different types of turtles. Did you know that sighting a turtle is considered to be a very lucky omen in any ancient culture ? . Turtle is symbol of patience, perseverance, peace & serenity.

We will take you through the amazing world of Turtles. They live in Sea, Lakes, Rivers and even in Wells. They live for hundreds of years and they do not need much food. Their physical needs are minimal.

If you really want to have a great experience of being with Turtle , you can do Scuba diving or Snorkelling . Dubai, UAE has some amazing places where you can have this experience. This place is called Snoopy Island. It is located about 75 miles north of Dubai.
